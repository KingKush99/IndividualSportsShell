How to Play Sumo Reel Rumble

  1. Set your bet using the drop-up control below the reels.
  2. Grab the lever on the right with your eyes, then press the red push button to spin.
  3. Three matching sumo icons across any row is a win and adds to your streak.
  4. Higher-ranked sumo fighters pay more, but appear less often.
  5. Breaking your streak resets it to zero, so choose your bets wisely.
  6. Your fighter name comes from your Profile display name.
